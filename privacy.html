Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2025

Welcome to our app! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. Please read it carefully.

1. Data We Collect

Our app is primarily offline, with most data stored locally on your device. Below are the types of data we may collect:

  • Locally Stored Data:
    • Location Data: We may access your approximate location (e.g., to determine if you are in a park) for local app features. This data is stored in the local Isar database on your device and is not uploaded to any server.
    • Photos and Camera Data: If you use camera or photo gallery features, related data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted.
  • Subscription Data:
    • When you purchase a subscription through Google Play and RevenueCat, we may collect purchase-related data (e.g., order ID, purchase status). This data is processed by Google Play and RevenueCat to manage subscriptions.
  • Diagnostics Data:
    • We use Firebase Analytics or Sentry to collect anonymized crash logs and device information (e.g., device model, OS version) to improve app performance and stability.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: Location and camera data support local app features and are stored only on your device.
  • Subscription Management: Purchase data is used to process and manage your subscriptions, ensuring access to premium features.
  • App Improvement: Diagnostics data is used to analyze app performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We only share data with the following third parties when necessary:

  • Google Play: Processes subscription purchases, which may involve collecting financial data to complete transactions.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscriptions and processes purchase-related data.
  • Firebase Analytics / Sentry: Collects anonymized diagnostics data to monitor app performance.

We do not share your location, photos, or other local data with any third parties, nor do we sell any data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Local Storage: Location and camera data are stored in the local Isar database on your device and are not transmitted.
  • Encrypted Transmission: Subscription and diagnostics data are transmitted via encrypted connections (HTTPS) to Google Play, RevenueCat, or Firebase/Sentry.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights:

  • Access and Control: You can manage app permissions (e.g., location, camera) at any time through your device settings.
  • Data Deletion: You can delete all locally stored data (Isar database) by uninstalling the app. Subscription data can be managed through Google Play or RevenueCat support channels.

6. Data Retention

Local data (e.g., location, photos) is stored on your device only for as long as you use the app and is deleted upon uninstallation. Subscription and diagnostics data retention is governed by the privacy policies of Google Play, RevenueCat, and Firebase/Sentry, and we retain data only for the minimum necessary period.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. If we learn that we have unintentionally collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Please check back periodically for the latest information.
